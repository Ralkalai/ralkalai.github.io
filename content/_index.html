Hi! I’m Rafa (ʁa-fa) Alkalai. I’m currently an undergraduate student at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. More specifically I am specializing in the study of Biodiversity with a focus on Latin America and Tropical Systems. A proud member of the Faculty of Land and Food Systems, currently enrolled in the Global Resource Systems program.

When I’m not attending to my responsibilities as a full-time student I enjoy thinking about the plethora of the diversity of life on Earth. I love learning new things and talking to people so please reach out!
